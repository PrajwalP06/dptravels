Tsomgo Plan

Tsomgo Lake (3 days - 2 nights)

Day 1: Pick up at Bagdogra Airport/ NJP railway station - Gangtok 130km/4hrs 30min (Approx).
Day 2: Early morning tour to Tsomgo lake. Places to visit:-
  • Old Baba Mandir
  • New Baba Mandir
  • Tsomgo Lake
And then back to Gangtok.
Day 3: Drop to Bagdogra Airport/ NJP railway station according to schedule.





Gurudongmar Plan

Gurudongmar (4 days - 3 nights)

Day 1: Places to visit-
  • Lachen(9,400 ft, 124 km)
  • Twin Waterfall
  • Singik View Point
Day 2: Places to visit-
  • Thangu(13,500 ft, 31kms from Lachen)
  • Chopta Valley (13,200 ft)
  • Gurudongmar Lake (17,800 ft **ONLY INDIAN NATIONAL IS ALLOWED)
Day 3: Places to visit-
  • Yumthang Valley (11,800 ft)
  • Hot Spring
  • Back to Lachung
Day 4: Drop to Gangtok. Tour End.
