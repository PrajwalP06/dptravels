Himalayan Protocols

Zero Plastic State

Sikkim is strictly plastic-free. Disposable plastic water bottles are prohibited in North and East Sikkim. Please carry reusable bottles.

Cultural Respect

Maintain silence and dress modestly while visiting monasteries. Always seek permission before taking photographs of locals or sacred sites.

Eco-Etiquette

Do not litter. Carry your trash back to your vehicle or hotel. Help us keep the fragile Himalayan ecosystem pristine for future voyagers.
